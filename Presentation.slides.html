Dataset Overview¶

This Loan Data from Prosper (a peer-to-peer lending platform) contains 113,937 loan entries with 81 variables covering a range of loan terms, borrower demographics, and credit profiles. Loan entries listed before July 2009 were dropped and at the end of the wrangling processes, there were 76168 entries with 19 variables to explore

Debt Consolidation Loans¶

Debt consolidation loans account for about 70% of returns and 64% of all loan entries. It's vital to when people tend to apply for such loans. From the chart, it appears that folks want to put their house in order and consolidate all their debt in anticipation of a new year as there's a surge from October through January

Occupation Comparison¶

While healthworkers, salespeople and computer programmers are estimated to bring the largest returns to the firm with debt consolidation loans, computer programmers are less likely to default or miss a payment followed by salespeople (who are in the sweet middle) and then healthworkers.

Best Loan Term¶

It appears 3-year loans account for majority of debt consolidation loans defaulted or not paid back completely while 1-year loans are less likely to be defaulted. The 1-year Debt Consolidation loans should be marketed more to people in these professions from the end of September each year.
